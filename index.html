About Us

We are an upscale coffee shop located on the historical public square situated in the heart of the Twin Lakes.

Located in what used to be the Moorman Drug Store on the south-west corner of Public Square and West Main Street, we are honored to have the opportunity to breathe new life into the walls of our new home while keeping in touch with what it once was.

Historic buildings have a soul all their own, with stories that contribute to memorable experiences that are hard to replicate in a new space. This is why we love our location.

We look forward to seeing you in our doors! So please stop by, pull up a chair and enjoy your time with us during your stay!

- The Storehouse Staff
